Stay ahead in today’s competitive market with Flipkart’s advertising campaign

The Indian online retail industry has become increasingly competitive over the years, with more and more sellers joining e-commerce platforms in the hope of increasing their sales. This has led to a crowded marketplace where it can be difficult for sellers to stand out and attract customers.

To stay competitive in today’s fierce market, we keep our clients up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies and provide them with actionable insights. We aim to improve the loyalty of customers towards your brand.

Flipkart's sponsored ads and performance-based marketing solution allow sellers to advertise their products on the platform and reach a wider audience. This is done through targeted advertising, where sellers can select specific demographics, interests, and browsing behaviour of users on Flipkart to display their ads.

When a user searches for a product or browses a category, the algorithm identifies the keywords and displays relevant ads to the user. The ads can appear as sponsored products, or in search results. The sellers can then pay for these ads on a cost-per-click (CPC) basis, meaning they only pay when a user clicks on their ad.

At Finessse Interactive we aim to utilize Flipkart's advertising campaign to increase the brand awareness and visibility of your products.

Also, ads on Flipkart provide detailed analytics and reports on the performance of the ads, allowing sellers to optimize their campaigns and maximize their ROI.

How does PLA works

At Finessse, we are a top-notch display advertising company that utilizes display ad strategies and appealing campaigns to boost brand awareness and exposure. Here's why you should choose us for your display media advertising needs:

To create a PLA, a seller needs to
Select the product they want to promote through Flipkart-sponsored ads
Set a bid amount for each click.
The bid amount is the maximum amount the seller is willing to pay for each click on their ad
The higher the bid amount, the more likely the ad will appear at the top of the search results

Once the bid amount is set, Flipkart's algorithm determines which ad to display based on several factors like

Bid Amount
Relevance of the product to the search query
And the historical performance of the ad.

The ad with the highest bid amount and the best relevance to the search query is typically displayed at the top of the search results.

Overall, Flipkart PLAs are an effective way for sellers to increase product/brand awareness and drive more sales on the platform. The CPC model ensures that sellers only pay when a customer clicks on their ad, making it a cost-effective advertising option.
