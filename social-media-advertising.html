Social Media Advertising

Social media marketing services can be a useful tool to reach a targeted audience and achieve real business results. With organic reach becoming increasingly difficult to achieve, investing in social media advertising can help ensure that your content is seen by the right people.

Social media advertising offers a range of options for businesses to connect with their target audience, from sponsored posts to display ads, to paid search ads. Each social media platform has its unique advertising options, allowing businesses to tailor their approach to the platform and the audience they want to reach.

When using social media marketing services, it's important to set clear goals and track metrics to ensure you are getting the most out of your investment. This can include tracking engagement, click-through rates, and conversions.

Finessse Interactive provides you with a well-rounded marketing approach including a mix of both paid and organic efforts to boost your reach. We also aim to build a sturdy and engaged audience.

Dominate Social Media With A Killer Advertising Campaign!

We have all heard the famous saying that “Failing to plan is planning to fail”. Plan your goals at an early stage and prepare yourself for future outcomes. Setting goals is a crucial step to ensure that your business is obtaining the maximum value from social media platforms. A blueprint of objectives is all you need to stay ahead in the world of social media advertising.

We at Finessse can help you to outline some steps that can help to set up effective social media goals. Let’s project some light on 5 hyper-efficient goals and how can you track them:

Shoot Up Your Brand Awareness Among Customers

Brand awareness is indeed one of the top motives why marketers use social media ad services. With the vast number of people spending a considerable amount of time on social media platforms every day, it has become one of the most effective ways for businesses to reach and engage with their target audience.

Moreover, social media platforms provide businesses with valuable insights and metrics that allow them to quantify the impact of their social media campaigns.

  • Followers count is a measure of how many people have chosen to follow a particular social media account, indicating the potential reach of that account.
  • The reach of social media posts refers to the number of unique users who have seen a specific social media post. This metric measures the actual exposure of a post and can indicate how effective it is in reaching the intended audience.
  • Mentions, shares, and retweets are metrics that measure how many people are talking about a brand or sharing its social media content.

Get More Eyes On Your Website.

Driving traffic from social media to a business's website or blog is a common goal for many marketers. By sharing content on social media platforms and including links to their website or blog, businesses can attract potential customers and increase their website traffic.

Social media marketing services are a valuable distribution channel for content because they provide businesses with the opportunity to reach a wider audience.

A few metrics to measure social media traffic:

  • Traffic from social media refers to the number of visitors who arrive at a website or blog through social media channels. With this businesses can determine which social media channels are generating the most traffic.
  • The bounce rate of social media traffic refers to the percentage of visitors who leave a website or blog after viewing only one page, without interacting further or navigating to other pages.
  • Clicks on social media posts tell you the number of times users click on a link included in a social media post. High click-through rates are a positive indicator that users are engaged with the content and are interested in learning more

Google Analytics is a powerful tool that provides businesses with valuable insights into their website traffic, including traffic from social media.

Not sure about which platform to choose? We at Finessse can help you break these technical barriers and guide you the most suitable strategy.

Lead Generation Fuels Business Growth.

Lead generation from social media marketing services involves ads using various social media platforms to attract potential customers and convert them into leads. This process typically involves creating engaging content, promoting it through social media advertising, and using various tactics such as contests, giveaways and calls to action to encourage users to provide their contact information. Once a user provides their contact information, they become a lead.

These metrics help businesses quantify their social media lead generation efforts, identify areas for improvement, and adjust their strategy accordingly to maximize their ROI.

1.Engage your audience, ignite your brand with social media

Improving customer engagement is essential for building a loyal customer base and increasing brand awareness. It involves encouraging visible interactions of users with your brands on social media, such as likes, comments, and shares. By improving engagement, businesses can increase the qualitative interactions with their audience, which can ultimately lead to increased brand loyalty, higher conversion rates, and improved customer satisfaction.

Here are a few more ways to calculate engagement rates:

  • Engagement rate by impressions (ERI). The percentage of people who interacted with your content out of the total number of times it was viewed.
  • Engagement rate by followers (ERF). The percentage of your followers who engaged with your content.
  • Total engagement rate (TER). The total number of engagements (likes, comments, shares, etc.) is divided by the total number of followers.

2.Make your customers feel heard and valued through your customer service

Customer service is crucial for social media branding because it directly impacts the way customers perceive and interact with a brand on social media. Failing to meet these expectations can result in negative reviews, complaints, and even viral backlash, which can damage a brand's reputation and impact its bottom line. On the other hand, providing excellent customer service on social media can improve customer satisfaction, foster brand loyalty, and even attract new customers through positive word of mouth. Improving customer service on social media is crucial for retaining existing customers and building brand loyalty.

Here are a few ways to improve your customer service on social media:

  • Respond to all customer inquiries within a certain time frame (e.g. 24 hours).
  • Increase the number of positive customer reviews on social media.
  • Resolve a certain percentage of customer issues on the first interaction.
  • Increase the number of followers who engage with your customer service content.
  • Decrease the number of negative mentions or complaints on social media.
